What is QuizConnect™?

QuizConnect™ is a web-based team-based quiz platform. The quiz game calls for cooperation within the whole team, since the answer to your question may very well be only visible to a teammate, but hurry - time is running out. The short timer takes the speed up a notch, but even if you are fast enough, the rising difficulty will take it to the next level and your knowledge with it.
